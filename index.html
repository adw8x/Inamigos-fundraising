Who We Are

Think Global, Act Local.

NayePankh Foundation is a non-governmental organisation born from the COVID-19 pandemic. Founded on 28th March 2021 by a group of high-schoolers in Kanpur, Uttar Pradesh, we have grown into one of India's biggest student-led NGOs — active in Kanpur, Ghaziabad, and more cities.

Our name 'NayePankh' means 'New Wings' — because we believe every underprivileged person deserves the wings to fly.

We are 12A and 80G certified, meaning your donations receive 50% tax relief under the Indian Income Tax Act. Featured in The Pioneer, Dainik Jagran and Hindustan, our work speaks for itself.

Our Ongoing Projects

Every initiative is built to create real, measurable change.

🍱

Free Food Distribution

We serve hot nutritional meals daily to pavement dwellers and underprivileged families. Because no one should go to bed hungry. We also feed stray animals — because compassion has no limits.

Learn More →
📚

Pathshala — Education Drive

Our mobile tutoring units staffed by student volunteers conduct community classes for street children of daily wage workers. We distribute stationery bags, textbooks, and writing pads — because education is the most powerful weapon against poverty.

Learn More →
🌸

Sanitary Napkin Campaign

We distribute biodegradable sanitary napkins and run menstrual hygiene awareness campaigns in rural areas. Because menstrual health is not a luxury — it is a right.

Learn More →
👕

Clothes & Blanket Drive

We provide warm winter blankets and clothes to poor families and pavement dwellers. Protecting them from the harsh Indian winters with dignity and warmth.
